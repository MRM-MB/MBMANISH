Project Overview

Living in Denmark, a country that uses bikes as a main mode of transport, my daily commute to university involves using a bicycle. Unfortunately, after an accident with an electric scooter, my rear light attachment broke, and I needed a holder to attach my back light.

Not wanting to buy a replacement and preferring to create my own solution, I designed a custom holder for my specific light, the OMERIL LD320 Rear Light.

Installation

To attach the holder to the bike's rear rack or luggage carrier, I used zip ties passed through the elongated hole opening. This ensures a secure fit that stays in place during rides.

Design & Compatibility

The holder has a square attachment of 17x17 mm designed specifically for my light. However, the model can be repurposed for other back attachments. It might need modifications to be scaled for your specific measurements, which can easily be done in slicer programs such as Bambu Studio, PrusaSlicer, or many others.
