Text Animations & Blender Basics

  • 3D Models & Animations
12 May 2019

These animations were done in Blender 2.79. It was my first exposure to model designs and animation, and my main focus was experimenting with text animation, lighting, and camera work, as well as physics and simulations to get my foot in the door to learn Blender.

As you can see in this section, I present some of my initial animations with this program that I learned with my high school professor Jesus Alvaro (@jesuslalvaro). I also learned some techniques and simulations from various YouTube channels that helped me develop animation skills and understand Blender better moving forward with my own creations:
