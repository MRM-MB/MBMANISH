These animations were created in Blender 2.79. They marked my first exposure to 3D animation, where I focused on mastering the fundamentals. My experiments primarily explored text animation, lighting, Blender modifiers, camera work, physics, and simulations.

As you can see in this section, I present some of my initial animations with this program that I learned with my high school professor Jesús Álvaro (@jesuslalvaro).
